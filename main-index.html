About Us

Having started in 2001, Halcyon High school has been existing for now over 20 years, providing quality education to Ugandans particularly Eastern Uganda. The years have been years of growth and expansion of a secondary school located at OTATAI-ASURET just 4KM from Soroti town on Old Mbale Road with all the facilities expected of an excellent secondary school Over years many are surprised by the steady rise of Halcyon High school.
